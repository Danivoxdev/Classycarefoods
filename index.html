Questions, Answered

Frequently Asked

What ingredients are in Tombrown Cereal?

Our blend includes maize, sorghum, millet, soybeans, groundnuts, crayfish, dates and a curated mix of seeds — all 100% natural, with no preservatives, sugar or artificial additives.

Is it safe for babies and children?

Yes. Tombrown is suitable from 6 months and up. It's smooth, easy to digest, and rich in nutrients that support healthy growth and weight gain.

How long does delivery take?

Lagos & Abuja deliveries arrive within 1–2 business days. Nationwide orders typically arrive in 3–5 business days via trusted dispatch partners.

How much does shipping cost?

Shipping is calculated based on your location and order size. Our team will confirm the exact fee on WhatsApp before you make payment.

Do you offer discounts for bulk orders?

Absolutely. Our 5kg Jumbo Pack already offers the best price per kilogram, and resellers or institutions can request custom wholesale pricing on WhatsApp.

How should I store the cereal?

Keep it sealed in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Once opened, reseal tightly — the bucket and sachet packs are designed to preserve freshness for months.
